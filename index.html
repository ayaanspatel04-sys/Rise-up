How It Works

Simple. Free. Online.

All classes are held live over video — no downloads, no subscriptions, no fees. Here's all it takes to get started.

01

Register Free

Fill out the short form below with your name, email, and which class you want. That's it — no income requirements, no background check.

02

Get Your Link

We email you a private video link before your class starts. Works on any phone, tablet, or computer — no app needed.

03

Attend Live

Join your class live with a certified professional instructor. Ask questions, get real answers, and interact with a small group of peers.

04

Keep Learning

Every class comes with a follow-up resource guide. Book a free 1-on-1 session with your instructor if you need extra support.

Reentry & Life Skills Track

Rebuilding After Incarceration — Online, at Your Pace

Returning home after incarceration means facing housing barriers, employment hurdles, and financial invisibility — all at once. Our reentry track connects you with licensed professionals who understand these challenges firsthand.

Every class in this track is led by a certified reentry specialist, licensed social worker, or legal professional — not general advisors. Real expertise for real obstacles.
